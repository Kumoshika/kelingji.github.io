Introduction: The Among Us: Red’s Diary impostor adventure series uses storybook diaries to launch readers into space—with a murderer!
Six astronauts. Two pets. One impostor…

That’s why it’s so puzzling when things start to go terribly wrong.
Crew members are being attacked one by one, and it’s clear the impostor won’t stop until no one on the Gos is left standing.
Can the crew find the culprit and put a stop to the impostor’s nefarious plan—or is the mission doomed?
